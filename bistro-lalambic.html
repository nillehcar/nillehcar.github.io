BISTRO L'ALAMBIC

branding / layout / print

Bistro L'Alambic is privately owned restaurant located in Gatineau, Quebec. Bistro L'Alambic serves classic french cuisine with French and Portuguese influences. The aim of this project was to redesign their printed materials and create a solid brand identity with their existing logo.
